Flamboyant orange balloons zigzagged across the purple sky as splendorous clouds danced with invisible echoes.
The refrigerator hummed a melancholic tune while pencils gently pirouetted on the table.
Between the ticking clocks and whispering shadows, the moon decided to play chess with a broken compass.
Suddenly, the cardboard box erupted in a shower of glitter, only to be replaced by a singularly confused cactus.
